ToS Scanner

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Summary: ToS Scanner reads Terms of Service text on your behalf, sends it to our AI for analysis, and returns a plain-language summary. We do not sell your data. We do not track your browsing history.

1. Who We Are

ToS Scanner ("we", "our", "us") is a Chrome extension that uses AI to summarize Terms of Service documents and flag potentially dangerous clauses. Our backend is hosted at tosscan-full-production.up.railway.app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Terms of Service text: When you trigger a scan, we extract visible text from the current page and send it to our server for AI analysis. We do not store this text after analysis is complete.
  • Google Account information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture to create your account.
  • Usage data: We log basic request metadata (timestamps, scan counts) to operate the service and prevent abuse.

We do not collect your browsing history, passwords, financial data, or any information from pages you do not explicitly scan.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To perform the ToS analysis you requested.
  • To manage your account and subscription (via Stripe).
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of our AI summaries.
  • To send transactional emails related to your account (no marketing without consent).

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third parties only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Google OAuth – for account sign-in. Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) – the ToS text you scan is sent to Anthropic's API for analysis. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Stripe – for payment processing. We never see or store your full card details. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Railway – our cloud hosting provider. Railway Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Scanned ToS text is processed in real time and not stored permanently. Account information (name, email) is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

ToS Scanner is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
